Why Choose Us?

In our Guest House you will feel like home far away from home.

Come as a guest - Leave as a friend

Let yourself be impressed by the warmth of the locals and make new friends.

Extremely familiar and flexible

We only have small groups and organize our daily program according to our guests' preferences.

Terrace with sunset view

The Kalhu Muraka Guest House has the best location on Keyodhoo. On our terrace with sea view you enjoy the daily breakfast and wonderful sunsets.

Safety first when diving

Our diving center is led by extremely professional PADI staff and perfectly maintained equipment.

Introducing Vimeo Music Store from Vimeo Staff on Vimeo.

Our Daily excursions

Every day we take you out on our dhoani (local boat) and bring you to the most beautiful places in the Vaavu atoll. There are uninhabited islands, sand banks or breath taking corral reefs where to discover the underwater beauty by snorkeling or diving.

Ambaraa

Bodumohooraa

Fotteyo

Fotteyo Finolhu (sand bank)

Hulidhoo

Alimatha Finolhu (sand bank)
